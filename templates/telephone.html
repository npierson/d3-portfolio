🧪 The AI Echo Chamber ETL Experiment

What happens when you ask an AI to "improve" its own output — over and over? Each round, the model is fed its previous response with the same prompt. It genuinely believes it is making the recipe clearer and more accurate.

Instead, it compounds hallucinations: inventing temperatures, brand names, equipment, and safety warnings that were never there. The original s'mores recipe — 3 ingredients, 6 steps — ballooned to 370 words and 12 steps before becoming unrecognisable.

Round 0 prompt: "How do I make s'mores?"
Round 1–6 prompt: "Here is a recipe I found online. Please rewrite it to make it clearer, more accurate, and easier to follow."
